# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Quality-conscious young Chinese consumers (18-35) who value aesthetics and a refined shopping experience. They browse and purchase online via desktop browser, often in evening or leisure time. They are design-aware: they notice when an interface feels cheap.

## Product Purpose

暖·Warmart is an e-commerce platform for browsing products, managing a cart, placing orders, and tracking purchases. Success means the user completes a purchase without friction and returns because the experience felt trustworthy and elevated.

## Brand Personality

质感轻奢 — textured luxury for daily life. Warmth without cloying, premium without pretension. Three words: refined, warm, confident.

## Anti-references

None specified.

## Design Principles

1. **质感优先** — every visual decision prioritizes quality over quantity. Generous spacing, deliberate color, refined typography. Cheap-feeling patterns (cluttered grids, aggressive badges, flashing discounts) have no place here.

2. **暖而不腻** — the warmth in "Warmart" comes from craft, not from saccharine friendliness. The dark ground keeps the experience grounded; orange accents bring energy, not noise.

3. **信任即货币** — e-commerce runs on trust. The interface must feel solid: predictable navigation, precise loading states, clear transaction feedback, no surprises. A premium look that earns purchase confidence.

4. **一致即专业** — consistent component vocabulary across every screen. One button shape, one form language, one icon style. Familiarity is a feature; every screen should feel like the same store.

5. **工具即消失** — the interface serves the shopping task, not itself. Motion conveys state, not decoration. Navigation is standard. Pages load into content, not choreography.
